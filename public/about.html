About Vinod Kurup

Welcome to my home page! (Yes, I know it’s 2012, but I still call it my home page). I’m probably the luckiest guy in the world, with a beautiful wife and 3 sweet, life-loving kids to prove it. I am a hospitalist working at a community hospital in North Carolina. I’m a Milwaukee Malayalee who hopes to retire in NYC someday. I am also a (free software) programmer, writer, runner, foodie, homeowner, reader, investor, in addition to about a hundred other things. I’m basically a wannabee Renaissance Man.
