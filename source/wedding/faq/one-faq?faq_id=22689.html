  1. Q: What is the Mehndi/Sangeet?

    A: It's a party on the day before the wedding. It traditionally was a time that the women would come together and sing songs, while painting their hands with Henna. We're equal opportunity party-ers now, so the men are invited. Any women wishing to get their hands painted are welcome to. Everyone will be able to enjoy the music, singing, food and drink at the beautiful Puri house.

  2. Q: What is the dress code for the Mehndi/Sangeet?

    A: Mala and Vinod will be wearing traditional Indian dress. There is no dress code for our guests, but we're guessing most people will be business casual. We encourage anyone who wishes to wear traditional Indian clothing to do so. Contact us if you need help!

  6. Q: Where did you get your invitations made?

    A: In India (New Delhi). We searched online and found something that we really liked. Mala's Dad and Ethan went to India in January and searched through countless stores, but couldn't find one that was quite close enough. Finally, at the last store that they visited, they found the exact invitation that we wanted. Thanks to them for all the hard work!
