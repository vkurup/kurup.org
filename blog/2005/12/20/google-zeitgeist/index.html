Google Zeitgeist

Every year, Google does an analysis of search queries over the past year. They call it the Google Zeitgeist. It’s interesting to see how ‘what we search for’ is a very accurate historical record of our times. The Apple iPod takes up 4 of the top 10 searches on Froogle, Google’s consumer search engine.

If you liked Zeitgeist, you can monitor weekly updates.
